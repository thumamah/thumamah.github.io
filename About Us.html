About Us

This site is in dedication to the unique and wonderful city of Dubai and all it has to offer. From world class beaches, hotels, luxury shopping and unique experiences hard found elsewhere, there is something on offer in Dubai for everyone. It is our pleasure to help bring Dubai to the forefront of discussion and recognition for what it is, a truly exciting metropolitan City that is a must visit.

We also have to draw attention to the defining feature of Dubai, it's ultramodern architecture resulting in a distinct skyline. Burj Khalifa, an 830m-tall tower, dominates the skyscraper-filled skyline, helping Dubai to hold the title of the worlds tallest skyline. On a visit here be sure to travel to the top to get a view from the worlds second tallest observation deck and take in the skyline with a backdrop of magnificant coastline. At its foot lies Dubai Fountain, with jets and lights choreographed to music. On artificial islands just offshore is Atlantis, The Palm, a resort with water and marine-animal parks. Dubai's modern take on classic Islamic architecture has helped to seperate it from much of the rest of the wrold.

Visit the rest of the site to delve deeper into what Dubai has to offer. We hope that we have inspired you to take a once in a lifetime trip to the Arab Emirates and visit it's crown jewel. If you decide to take the leap, we have recomendations for flights, accomodation and activites once you touch down.
